Does Robert Exhibit Criteria for PTSD? Based on your discussion with Robert, do you believe he exhibits any of the criteria for PTSD? If so, what signs do you think he demonstrates?

Possible criteria of PTSD in this case include:

  • Robert’s 4 deployments to high conflict zones (possible Crit. A)
  • Possible startle response in waiting room (Crit. E)
  • Disrupted sleep (Crit. E)
  • Avoiding leaving the house (Crit. C)
  • Anxiety/panic on exposure to certain racial groups (Crit. B)
  • Stomach ache and vomiting when thinks of leaving the house (Crit. B)
  • Negative beliefs and expectations that people in crowds are dangerous (Crit. D, E)
  • Binge drinking (Crit. C)

 

Diagnostic Criteria for PTSD

  1. Exposure to actual or threatened death, serious injury or sexual violence
  2. Presence of at least one intrusion symptom
  3. Persistent avoidance of stimuli associated with the event
  4. Negative cognitions or moods associated with the event
  5. Marked alterations in arousal or reactivity after the event
  6. Duration of the symptoms
  7. Functional significance
  8. Exclusion criteria
