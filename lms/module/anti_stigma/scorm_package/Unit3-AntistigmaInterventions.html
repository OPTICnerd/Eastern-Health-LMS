Antistigma Interventions

There are five common approaches to antistigma interventions. Contact-based education through facilitating interaction between citizens or health providers with persons with mental illness and lived experience has been shown effective. Also structural change of how social structures or organizations function can be effective in leading to change. This includes aligning and changing necessary laws, policies, and practices to improve equity and social justice for persons with mental health problems.

  1. Structural change - Improve equity and social justice
  2. Contact-based education - Improve attitudes & behaviours
  3. Protest initiatives - Change offensive language or behaviour
  4. Traditional education - Improve literacy and help-seeking
  5. Social marketing campaigns - Improve awareness and attitudes
