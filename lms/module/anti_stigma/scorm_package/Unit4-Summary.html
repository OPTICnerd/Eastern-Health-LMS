Summary

  • Depression: a complex disorder
    • Depression is multidimensional
    • The lack of full recovery from a depressive episode can negatively influence the course of illness
  • Neurobiological underpinnings
    • MDD, pain, and stress are all associated with similar suppression of neurotrophic factors and compromised neuroplasticity
    • brain derived neurotrophic factor (BDNF) may be altered in depression and pain, leading to structural and functional changes
  • Multidimensional treatment
    • Treatment may ameliorate functional, and thus structural, changes in the brain
    • Activation of 5HT (serotonin),and/or NE (norepinephrine), and/or DA (dopamine) may restore neuroprotective benefits, neuroplasticity and neurogenesis
    • Guideline recommended treatment of MDE includes Psychotherapies (CBT-Cognitive Behaviour Therapy and IPT-Interpersonal Therapy) as well as Somatic Therapies (antidepressant medications, light therapy, ECT)
    • Empower patients to take an active in their illness and achieving optimal wellness.
