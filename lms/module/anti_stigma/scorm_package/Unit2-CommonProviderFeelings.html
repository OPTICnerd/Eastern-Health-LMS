Common Provider Feelings

Common Provider Feelings when treating persons with Mental Illness

Health Provider Feeling/Emotion

Due to:
Fear
  • Perceived or real danger
  • Unpredictability
  • Contagious
  • Psychic forces/spirits/magic
  • Limited clinical skills/knowledge
Anger
  • Illness behaviours, signs & symptoms (self-harm/suicide)
  • Cognitive deficits
  • Limited insight
  • Poor treatment adherence
  • Chronic relapses/revolving door
  • Documentation/paperwork/legal requirements
Bewildered
  • Strange thoughts
  • Bizarre odd behaviour
  • Weird themes – delusions
  • Disordered perceptions/hallucinations
Humour
  • Amusing signs & symptoms
  • Healthy coping mechanisms for provider discomfort
Empathy/Compassion
  • Professionalism
  • Patient & family suffering
  • Saving lives
  • Easing suffering
  • Educating others
  • Advocacy
  • Social responsibility
