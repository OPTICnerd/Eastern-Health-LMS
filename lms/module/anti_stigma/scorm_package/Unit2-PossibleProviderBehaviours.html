Possible Provider Behaviours

Possible provider behaviours towards persons with mental illness:

  • Avoidance
  • Social/professional distancing
  • Anger at mental health patients/care providers
  • Blaming
  • Lack of compassion
  • Desire to “dump” or “turf”
  • Low quality of care and medico-legal risk taking
  • Diagnostic Overshadowing
  • Lack of professionalism
