Reflection Question #1

Does Julie Exhibit Risk Factors for PTSD and complex PTSD? Summarize the relevant risk and prognostic factors that Julie has presented with.

 

Expert Suggested Answer (Click to reveal)

The relevant risk and prognostic factors that Julie presents include:

  • Chronic traumatization
    • Reports a history of childhood sexual abuse and multiple sexual assaults in adulthood
  • Young age at initial exposure to trauma
  • Initial trauma by a caregiver
  • Lack of social support during childhood trauma
    • Blamed by mother
  • Acting out coping strategies
    • Binge drinking
    • Self injury (cutting) to manage anxiety and emotional distress
  • Reckless or self-destructive behaviour
    • Binge drinking at bars
  • Self-blame, low self esteem.
