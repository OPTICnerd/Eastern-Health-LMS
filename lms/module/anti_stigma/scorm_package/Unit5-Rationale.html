Rationale for an Implementation Tool

Challenges to caring for mental health issues…

 

Family physicians:
  • Need more Mental Health training
  • 50% of their practice involves mental health patients
  • Even more when you consider that their chronic disease patients have twice the incidence of comorbid depression and anxiety

…leads to a lack of capacity and comfort

 

Results in:

  • Avoidance
  • less prevention
  • more crisis
  • more deterioration
  • more relapse
  • more fear
  • more stigma
  • vicious circle

 

What fights stigma?

  • Increase comfort
  • Increase confidence
  • Increase client participation
  • Decrease referral to Mental Health (MH) system
