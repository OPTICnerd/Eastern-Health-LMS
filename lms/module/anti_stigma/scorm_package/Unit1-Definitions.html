Definitions

Literacy is knowledge about symptoms of mental illnesses, their treatments & available services

 

Prejudice is a negative attitude that is resistant to change

 

Discrimination is unfair treatment that denies ones’ human rights

 

Stigma is a complex social process that is the culmination of prejudice and discrimination

  • a “mark of disgrace”
  • “an attribute that is deeply discrediting” and reduces the bearer “ from a whole and usual person to a tainted discounted one” (Erving Goffman 1963 p.3)
  • Stigmatized individuals possess (or are believed to possess) some attribute , or characteristic, that conveys a social identity that is devalued in a particular social context (Crocker et al. 1998 p.505)
  • Separates “us” from “them”
