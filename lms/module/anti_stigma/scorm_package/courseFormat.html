Course Format

It is recommended that you complete the course as follows:

  • Complete the Pre-Test in order to self-assess your familiarity with the course topics.
  • Work through the course content and case studies.
  • Respond to the discussion questions in the case study.
  • Explore the resources in the References & Resources section.
  • Complete the evaluation survey to provide us with feedback for continuous improvement.

Please keep in mind that you MUST complete the Post-Test to receive your CME credits.

 

Course Navigation

  • Click the arrows to move through the pages of the course.
  • Your progress through each section of the course is shown on the section progress bar.
  • Your progress through the course is bookmarked so you can choose to complete it over multiple sessions.
