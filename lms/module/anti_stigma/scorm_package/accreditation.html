Accreditation

In order to meet the requirements for this course and obtain your Mainpro M1 and Maintenance of Certification study credits, you must:

  • Complete all course content.
  • Complete the Post Test at the end of course.

Although not required, the following are strongly recommended:

  • Respond to all discussion questions.
  • Complete the course evaluation to ensure continuous course improvement.

Mainpro-M1

This course currently meets the accreditation criteria of the College of Family Physicians of Canada and has been accredited by Professional Development & Conferencing Services, Memorial University for up to 3.0 Mainpro-M1 credits.

 

Maintenance of Certification (Section 1)

This event is an Accredited Group Learning Activity (Section 1) as defined by the Maintenance of Certification program of The Royal College of Physicians and Surgeons of Canada, and approved by Memorial University for up to 3.0 credits.

 

Participants in this course may also elect to use these learning materials as part of a self-study learning activity.

 

The Linking Learning to Practice program of the College of Family Physicians of Canada and the self-learning (Section 2) category of the Maintenance of Certification program with the Royal College of Physicians and Surgeons of Canada both provide frameworks for learners to build on the learning experience presented here.
