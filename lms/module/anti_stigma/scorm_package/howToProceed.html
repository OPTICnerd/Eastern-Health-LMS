How to Proceed

It is recommended that you complete this course as follows:

  • Review the information contained in this “Course Information” section to ensure that you are familiar with the course format and layout.
  • Review the “Accreditation” section to ensure you complete all tasks required to earn credits.
  • Complete the “Pre-Test” self-assessment. You may complete the Pre/Post-Tests only once.
  • Work through the course content, and respond to the group discussion questions:
    • Group discussion activities are provided so that you can discuss aspects of the case with your peers;
    • If you have additional questions relating to the content, an “Ask the Expert” feature allows you to pose questions to the module’s subject matter expert. We will do our best to respond within 48 hours.
    • Click on in-text citations to view complete references. A numbered reference list is also provided on the “References” page.
  • Explore the “References” and “Additional Resources” section.
  • Complete the “Post-Test”. This is mandatory in order to receive your learning credits.
  • Please complete the “Course Evaluation” to provide us with feedback for continuous improvement.
