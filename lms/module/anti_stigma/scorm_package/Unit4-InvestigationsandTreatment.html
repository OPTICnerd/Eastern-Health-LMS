Investigations and Treatment

  • Rule out screen for other causes
    • Screen for comorbid mental health disorders
    • Lab-TSH, Glucose, CBC, electrolytes , Creatinine, B12, LFT’s, metabolic profile, drug screen
  • Previous records, discharge summaries
  • Interview family, partners, friends

Might consider:

  • Sleep study-look for shortened REM latency
  • EEG
  • CT scan head

Treatment

  • Psychotherapies-CBT/IPT
  • Pharmacotherapy-antidepressants

 

Key Clinical Messages about Major Depression (Ungar, T 2010)

  • It’s not your fault (it is not you it’s the depression)
  • It’s a real medical condition
  • It doesn’t mean you’re weak, or unintelligent, or not trying hard enough
  • It’s treatable, it can get better, help is available
  • You can do a lot yourself to manage your illness
