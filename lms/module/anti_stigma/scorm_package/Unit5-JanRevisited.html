Jane Revisited

Jan is a 58 year old married mother of two with a responsible full time job and a history of a previous episode of major depression. Jan has been calling the office frequently saying that for the last two months she is having problems sleeping, is feeling a lot of anxiety during the day and is finding she just can’t settle. She calls the office once again to make an appointment.

Jan reports:

  • Lost her job
  • Facing bankruptcy
  • Feeling tired all the time
  • Doesn’t want to do anything
  • Arguing with teenage son
  • Drinking more coffee
  • Having a few more beers
  • Not interested in seeing friends
  • Angry a lot about little things
  • Feel like a failure
  • Watching TV all night
  • Sleeping during the day
  • Arthritis pain and limitations
